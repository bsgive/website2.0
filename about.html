About Me:

Hi, I’m Ben Givens, a software engineer from CDA, Idaho. I specialize in designing and building efficient, user-focused applications with clean code and modern technologies.

Outside of coding, I’m passionate about continuous learning and staying up to date with the latest in tech. I also enjoy connecting with others in the field and collaborating on projects that make a meaningful impact.

I believe that no true problem cannot be solved through team work and personal dedication.
